Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

Welcome to Vitae. We prioritize your privacy and transparency above all else. This policy explains how we handle your data in our hybrid storage system.

1. Data Storage: Local vs. Cloud

A. Your Resume Data (Private & Local)

CRITICAL: All personal information you enter into your CV (Name, Experience, Education, etc.) is stored LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE ONLY. We do NOT sync your personal resume data to our clouds or keep copies of your private career details in a central database.

B. Template Designs & Shared Content (Cloud-Based)

  • Template Sync: Custom template layouts are stored in our cloud so you can use them across your devices.
  • Template Sharing: If you choose to SHARE a template with the community, only the layout and the generic text inside that template will be uploaded.

2. Data Deletion & Account Removal

Google requires us to provide clear ways for you to delete your data. We offer two methods:

A. In-App Performance

You can delete everything directly in the app: Settings > Delete Account. You can choose to delete your Template Designs, Community Postings, or your entire Account Identity.

B. Manual Request (Web Fallback)

If you cannot access the app, you may request full data erasure by emailing us. We will process all cloud deletion requests within 7 days.

3. AI Processing Disclaimer

The App uses third-party AI services (like Google Gemini) for text formatting. When using these features, your text is sent for processing. We do not control how these third-party providers use data to train their models.
