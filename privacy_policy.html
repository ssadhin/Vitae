Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

Welcome to Vitae. This policy outlines our commitment to your privacy and explains how we handle your data within the App.

1. Data Storage: Local vs. Cloud

A. Your Resume Data (Private & Local)

GURANATEED: All personal information you enter into your CV (Name, Experience, Education, etc.) is stored LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE ONLY. We do NOT sync your private resume entries to our servers.

B. Template Designs & Shared Content (Cloud-Based)

To enable cross-device features, template layout configurations are stored in our cloud. If you choose to SHARE a design with the community, only the design layout and any text you explicitly include in that shared version will be uploaded.

2. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted providers to deliver premium features:

  • Google AdMob: Manages advertisements within the App.
  • Google Play Billing: Handles secure payment for subscriptions.
  • AI Integration: We use third-party AI to assist with text formatting. Processing occurs on their servers; please refer to their respective policies regarding data usage.
