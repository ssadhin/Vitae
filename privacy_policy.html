Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 14, 2026

Welcome to Vitae. We prioritize your privacy and transparency above all else.

1. Data Storage: Local vs. Cloud

A. Your Resume Data (Private & Local)

NOTICE: All personal information you enter into your CV is stored LOCALLY ON YOUR DEVICE ONLY. We do NOT sync your private career details to our clouds.

B. Template Designs & Shared Content (Cloud-Based)

Our custom template designs are stored in our cloud to allow use across devices. If you choose to SHARE a template with the community, the design and any text intentionally included in that template will be uploaded.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted partners to provide core features:

  • Google AdMob: For showing relevant ads.
  • Google Play Billing: For secure premium subscriptions.
  • AI Services: We use third-party AI for text parsing. Be aware that these providers may use data for model training based on their own policies.
