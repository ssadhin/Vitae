Privacy Policy for Vitae
Effective Date: March 14, 2026

Welcome to Vitae ("the App"). We prioritize your privacy and transparency.

1. Data Storage: Local vs. Cloud
We use a hybrid approach to ensure your privacy while enabling community features:

A. Your Resume Data (Private & Local)
All personal information you enter into your CV (Name, Experience, Education, etc.) is stored locally on your device only.
We do not "sync" your personal resume data to our clouds or keep copies of your private career details in a central database.
B. Template Designs & Shared Content (Cloud-Based)
Template Sync: Our custom template designs and layout configurations are stored in our cloud to allow you to use them across devices.
Template Sharing: If you choose to share a template design you have created, the layout design and any text or information intentionally included within that template will be uploaded to our cloud to be shared with other users.
Please ensure you do not include sensitive personal information within a template design you intend to share publicly.
2. Third-Party Services
While your private resume data stays on your device, the App uses third-party services for specific features:

A. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Disclaimer
The App uses third-party AI services (such as Gemini or OpenAI) to help parse and format your resume text.

Data Usage: When you use AI features, the text you provide is sent to the AI service provider for real-time processing.
Training Warning: Please be aware that third-party AI providers may use the data sent to them to train their models. We have no control over their policies once data is sent for processing.
B. Other Third-Party Providers
The App includes the following Google services which may collect certain technical identifiers:

Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements.
Google Play Billing: Used for secure in-app purchases and subscriptions.
Firebase Authentication: (Optional) Used for secure login. Even with an account, your resume data remains on your device or in your private control.
3. Data Security
Since your resume data is stored on your device, its security depends on your device's security settings (like your passcode or biometric lock). We recommend keeping your device updated.

4. Your Rights
You have full control over your data. Because it is stored locally, you can delete your information at any time by clearing the App data or uninstalling the App.

5. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
