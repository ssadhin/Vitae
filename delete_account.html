Account Deletion & Data Removal

We provide clear and easy ways for you to delete your data and account to comply with our commitment to user privacy.

Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The fastest way to remove your data is through the Vitae app:

1 Open the Settings screen.
2 Tap the Delete Account button.
3 Select what you want to delete (Templates, Shared Content, or Account Identity).
4 Confirm the deletion. Your data will be wiped instantly from our cloud.

Option 2: Manual Request

If you have uninstalled the app or cannot access it, you can request manual deletion via email.

Data Deletion Request
Email us at: asanistudiobangladesh@gmail.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request for [Your UID or Email]

We process all manual deletion requests within 7 business days. Once deleted, your cloud data and membership cannot be recovered.
