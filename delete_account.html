Account & Data Removal

We offer transparent and immediate ways to delete your cloud-stored data and account identity.

Option 1: In-App Deletion

This is the fastest method. Deletion takes effect immediately:

1
Open Settings in the Vitae app.
2
Tap the Delete Account button.
3
Select the data types you wish to erase.
4
Confirm. Your data is wiped from our cloud storage instantly.

Option 2: Manual Data Erasure

If you no longer have the app installed, you can request manual removal.

Email Request
Submit your request to: asanistudiobangladesh@gmail.com

Requests are processed within 7 business days. Note: Local resume data on your device is only deleted by uninstalling the app.
